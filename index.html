About Us

What is E-Cell?

The E-Cell at Sri Aurobindo College aims to raise awareness about India’s growing entrepreneurial culture. Its goal is to inspire students to explore starting their own ventures. The society offers guidance and support to young entrepreneurs and their start-ups. Through mentorship and resources, we empower students to embrace innovation and drive growth.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to equip students with essential entrepreneurial skills, foster innovation, and develop ethical leaders. Through resources, workshops, and mentorship, we support creativity, collaboration, and problem-solving. Our goal is to empower the next generation of entrepreneurs to drive innovation and growth while upholding integrity and social responsibility.
